ABOUT ME_
My name is MengYang lee, a graphic designer originally from Taiwan, and I have stayed in San Francisco for education. I am currently living in New Taipei City. Most of my works are surrounded by UI/UX and graphic design, including design strategy, branding, and visual systems.

I am interested in creating designs that help people have smooth visual communication, and with communicating, I can solve problems and change people’s lives. Having the ability to solve the problem one by one is what I’m good at and proud of.

Besides my passion for graphic design, I also like to do illustration and photography. Traveling across the nation to shoot unique wildlife photography is essential in my life. And I am hoping to combine my interests into my profession to gain an even more exciting outcome.

If you are interested in my work or just what to have some conversion to know me better, please don’t hesitate to contact.
