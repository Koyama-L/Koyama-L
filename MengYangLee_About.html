ABOUT ME_
Hello, my name is Meng Yang Lee ,currently I am living in New Taipei City. I am a graphic designer, originally from Taiwan and have just finished my graduate studies in the School of Graphic Design and Digital Media at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco.

My projects include, UX/UI, design strategy, branding, and visual systems. I am interested in creating work that resonates with audiences, perhaps even making their lives better. Solving problems is what I am most interested in and proud of doing.

Besides my passion for graphic design, I also enjoy illustration and photography. Traveling across the nation to shoot unique wildlife photography is important to me. I am hoping to combine my interests into my design to create exciting outcomes.

If you are interested in my work or just want to chat and get to know me, please don’t hesitate to give me a call.

Thank you for thaking the time to look at my work.
