SEE THROUGH
In all things of nature
there is something of the marvelous.
OBJECTIVE_
The objective of this project is to redesign a coffee table book. A coffee table book’s purpose is to display on a table intended to entertain the guests. The main objective will be to find a way to display the amazing pictures in the book.

APPROACH_
I picked the book “Wonders: spectacular moments in nature photography” as my redesign pieces. By analyzing the context of the book, I narrow the target audience down to young designers, photographers, and wildlife lovers around the age of twenty to thirty-five. Then I start to use typography to assist the photography to be the hero of the book.
