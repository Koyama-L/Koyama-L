Knowing yourself is
the beginning of all wisdom.
CONCEPT_
Self-reflection is one of the most important ways to know yourself. Only by fully acknowledging what we are not capable of can we learn things that we lack, and only by acknowledging what we are capable of can we gain confidence and know who we are, and discover our true self.
This book and all the works included reflect who I am and what I’m capable of, and it is not the result of my journey; it is simply the milestone of who I am before I keep on my journey to progress as a graphic designer.
