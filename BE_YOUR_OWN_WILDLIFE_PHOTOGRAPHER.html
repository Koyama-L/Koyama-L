Résumé
Wildlife is around us,
it takes no professional to be part of nature.
OBJECTIVE_
Wildlife Diversity is the distinctive study related to various types of species existing in the ecosystem. The main objective is to gather a group of photographers to share their photos and stories between themselves and the wilderness to teach people our relationship with nature.

APPROACH_
My approach is to create a platform that is friendly to amateurs and detailed enough for professional photographers and encourages them to post their own stories about wild encounters, and by telling the stories, reminding us we are always a part of nature.
