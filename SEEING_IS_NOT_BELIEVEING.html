Résumé
The more I learn,
the more I realize how much I don't know.
OBJECTIVE_
How did the rumors spread? How can I teach people how to verify the information they see on the internet? Information on the internet can be confusing, but we highly rely on the internet to access information in recent days. Therefore emphasizing how media literacy is so critical is my main objective in this project.

APPROACH_
My approach is to create a brand that combines social media, service, and narrative pieces called Ensight. Ensight includes a fact-checking website with reliable studies and links, an immediate fact-checking service, and a Youtube channel that does weekly fact-checking videos. My brand helps our users to get information and avoid unverified information from content farms or other websites that provide false information.
